  • Purpose of the Project
    Our group is building a database for the nonprofit organization Pilots N Paws. Founded in 2008, Pilots N Paws connects members of the general aviation community with animals in need of transport to new adopted homes or shelters with no-kill policies in place. As an entirely volunteer organization, Pilots N Paws is essentially an ongoing rescue airlift of animals in need. Presently, pilots interested in signing up for a transport mission have to scour an online message board with hundreds of posts requesting for help transporting an animal. Our group intends to build a mobile app to allow pilots to browse, sign up for, and discuss trips
  • User Types (Personas)
    • Shelter Coordinators
      A Shelter Coordinator is someone who works in an animal shelter coordinating the adoption of animals out of the shelter or intake of homeless animals into the shelter. At times, someone may adopt an animal from a location far from the shelter. In this situation, the only way the adoption can take place is if there is safe transportation for the animal to the adopted home. Pilots N Paws provides a means for acquiring this transportation. In addition to adoptions, some shelters must euthanize animals not adopted after a certain period of time due to space and resource considerations. In this situation, a Shelter Coordinator can request a “freedom flight” for a Pilots N Paws volunteer pilot to pick up the animal and relocate it to a new shelter arranged by the coordinator. In both of these situations the Shelter Coordinator can use the Pilots N Paws mobile app to post these trip requests and keep up to date on the status of their open trips
    • Volunteer Pilot
      Volunteer Pilots choose to fly trips for Pilots N Paws for many different reasons. Whether it is because they are looking for a reason to take out their airplane on the weekend, their love of animals, or a tax write-off the mission of Pilots N Paws could not be accomplished without them. Pilots N Paws was founded in part because small airplanes are perfectly suited for comfortably transporting animals as opposed to commercial airliners. In addition to the opportunity to fly and help animals, pilots are a community oriented group of people, and Pilots N Paws provides a means to meet other pilots and share experiences
